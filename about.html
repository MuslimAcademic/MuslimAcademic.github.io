About This Website

The purpose of this website is to help those who want to seek Islamic knowledge according to the traditional progression of books, which is the path to Islamic scholarship, as mentioned by the scholars of Saudi Arabia like Shaykh Uthaymeen and others.

Please note that ideally the student would study the basics with a Shaykh before moving on to researching advanced topics on his own. However this website is for those who do not have access to the scholars but are still eager to learn.
